About

I design products that feel inevitable.

I started writing code in 2004, founded my first agency in 2008, and got a golden ticket to Google in 2014. Since then I've worked across the full spectrum — from McKinsey boardrooms mapping 9-stakeholder election processes, to PwC applying behavioural science to BI workflow adoption, to building life-critical AI platforms entirely solo.

My edge is the combination of deep user empathy, systems-level product thinking, and the ability to build what I design. I don't wait for a brief — I write it, prototype it, and ship it. Currently operating at 10x velocity using AI tooling (Claude + Cursor + AntiGravity) to prototype and build resilient, LLM-enhanced user experiences. I don't just write code; I own the end-user delight.

I'm particularly drawn to products where the interaction model itself is the innovation — agentic AI systems, voice interfaces, multi-device real-time experiences. Places where getting the design wrong has real consequences, and getting it right changes how people live or work.
